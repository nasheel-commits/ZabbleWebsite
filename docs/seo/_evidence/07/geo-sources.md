## Notes on accuracy
- ACFE: **$145,000 is the median** loss per case; the "$1.5M+ per case" figure in
  ACFE's headline is the **average**. We use the median + the 5%-of-revenue + the
  12-months figures (the unambiguous, headline statistics).
- POPIA: R10m / 10 years is the **upper tier** (serious offences, s107(a)); a lesser
  tier is R1m / 12 months (s107(b)). We cite the upper tier and name the Act + year.
- McKinsey 60%/30%: a statement about *automatable activities*, not jobs eliminated —
  we use it as such ("activities", not "jobs").
- Gartner quote is attributed to a named analyst (Alan Antin) and a dated press
  release. Gartner's site blocks automated fetches; the prediction + quote are
  corroborated across multiple secondary reports (MediaPost, Search Engine Land).
- IDC "Data Age 2025" is the origin of both the ~80%-unstructured and the
  data-created-vs-analysed framing; we attribute to IDC + year.
