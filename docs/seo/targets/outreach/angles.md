# Pitch Angles — tied to Zabble systems & stories (owned by S04)

Every outreach needs a reason to exist. These angles map a prospect type to a
concrete Zabble asset or story, so the pitch leads with value. Pair each with a
template in `email-templates.md` and an anchor type from `anchor-guidance.md`.

## 1. The reference asset (broadest — for media, resource pages, guest hosts)
- **Hook:** *"The South African Operations-Software Landscape"* —
  `/insights/south-african-operations-software-landscape`. A free, citable map of the
  operational-software categories SA businesses run and where bespoke fits.
- **Use for:** BusinessTech, MyBroadband, ITWeb, resource pages, "best software"
  roundups.
- **Why it lands:** journalists want a framework and a link, not a sales pitch.

## 2. The build-vs-buy POV (op-ed / commentary)
- **Hook:** the five build-vs-buy questions (from the landscape asset). A founder
  op-ed: *"Most business software should be bought. Here's the 10% you should build."*
- **Use for:** ITWeb, Ventureburn, TechCentral, founder newsletters.

## 3. Industry outcome stories (vertical media + associations)
Tie to a specific system + the documented outcome:
- **Legal:** Document Intelligence — "intake from forty minutes to under four seconds"
  (`/systems/document-intelligence`, `/industries/legal`).
- **Financial services:** Compliance Reporting — "quarter-end from a fortnight to
  hours, every figure traceable" (`/industries/financial-services`).
- **Logistics:** Inventory Clarity — "cycle counts from a day to ten minutes"
  (`/industries/logistics`).
- **Manufacturing:** Predictive Maintenance — "one prevented breakdown pays for the
  system" (`/industries/manufacturing`).
- **Use for:** vertical/trade publications, industry associations, partner blogs.

## 4. Local angle (city media / directories)
- **Hook:** "A South African firm building custom systems for {city} businesses" +
  the relevant city page (`/locations/{city}`).
- **Use for:** regional media, city directories, chambers of commerce.

## 5. Partner / co-build angle
- **Hook:** the Kairos co-build with KnvilLabs; vendor showcases where Zabble uses a
  tool well.
- **Use for:** partner sites, vendor customer-story pages.

## 6. Data angle (forward-looking, needs original data)
- **Hook:** a small original SA dataset — e.g. a 50-respondent survey on where SA
  operators lose the most hours to manual work — published into the landscape asset.
- **Use for:** the strongest media placements; build once B-tier relationships exist.
- **Note:** any stat must be real and sourced (conventions §6). Do not pitch invented
  numbers.
