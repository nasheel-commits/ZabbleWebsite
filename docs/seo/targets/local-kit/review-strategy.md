# Review-Generation — operational checklist (owned by S04)

The strategy and benchmark live in `../local-seo.md` §5. This is the run-it version.

**Benchmark** (Maps, "software company", 2026-06-04): comparable SA consultancies
sit at BBD 4.6 (249), Entelect 4.7 (179), CPI 4.7 (157)
[`../../_evidence/04/business-listings__software-company-jhb.json`,
`..._cpt.json`]. **Target:** 20–30 reviews at ≥4.7 in the first two quarters.

## The loop
1. **Trigger at delivered value.** When a system ships and the outcome lands, ask
   that stakeholder for a review. Specific outcome → specific, quotable review (also
   feeds GEO).
2. **Make it one tap.** Generate the GBP short review URL; put it in the email
   signature, project-closeout email, and invoice footer.
3. **Sequence:** kickoff → midpoint → closeout request → one gentle follow-up.
4. **Spread:** most to GBP; seed 2–3 on Clutch/GoodFirms for B2B trust.
5. **Respond to every review** using the brand name (signal + GEO corroboration).
6. **Never incentivise** (Google policy). **POPIA:** only contact clients you have a
   lawful basis to; keep requests transactional. Coordinate with B4 (analytics/privacy).
