## Answer-first intro spec
*"Lead qualification automation runs the first conversation with every inbound
enquiry — web form, WhatsApp, voicemail, chat — extracts a qualifying brief
(intent, scope, timing, budget, urgency), and routes accordingly: high-value
leads to senior reps with context attached, price-shoppers to self-serve,
out-of-scope to a polite redirect. Reps stop sorting the inbox and start working
the leads worth working."* Then the arc.
