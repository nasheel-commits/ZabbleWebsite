## Answer-first intro spec
*"Regulatory reporting automation means a system that knows which of your data
sources each submission draws from, pulls the figures itself, validates them
against the regulator's rule pack, and traces every number back to its source
row. For POPIA filings, SARB returns, or donor reports, it turns a quarter-end
scramble into an answer auditors get in seconds."* Then the arc.
