## Answer-first intro spec
*"Operational analytics turns the data your business already generates into a
clear picture of what's happening and what to do next — shaped for the person
making the decision, at the moment they make it. The failure mode isn't missing
data; it's a dashboard so generic that four people open it and close it no wiser."*
Then the arc.
