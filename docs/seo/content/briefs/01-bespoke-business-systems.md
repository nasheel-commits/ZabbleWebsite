## Why this is the thesis pillar
This is the page that defines the Zabble entity for AI engines: *a South African
firm that builds bespoke operational systems instead of selling software.* It
sits above all four pillar hubs and is the most-linked-to internal page. Get the
liftable, declarative claims right here and every downstream article corroborates them.

## Answer-first intro spec (first 40–60 words, lift-ready)
Open by answering the title in 1–2 declarative sentences, e.g.: *"A bespoke
business system is software built around one company's actual workflow — not a
generic product it has to bend to fit. It beats off-the-shelf tools when the
problem costing you the most doesn't match what any product was designed to
solve."* Then the problem → what we build → what changes arc.
