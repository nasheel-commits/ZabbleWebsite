## Answer-first intro spec
*"Business process automation means handing the repetitive, rule-based parts of
a workflow — the weekly spreadsheet, the copy-paste between two tools, the
follow-up nobody got to — to a system, so your people do only the work that
needs a human. For South African businesses it's less about replacing staff than
about plugging the leaks that quietly cost time and money every week."* Then the arc.
