## Role in the model
The Audit Trails / governance hub. Distributes to every module where "the audit
trail is the by-product of running the work" — approval, case management,
compliance reporting, accounting, reconciliation, decision engine. ZA regulatory
framing is the moat: global SaaS rarely speaks FAIS/POPIA/SARB.

## Answer-first intro spec
*"An audit trail is a tamper-evident record of who did what, when, and why —
captured automatically as work happens, not reconstructed from email when a
regulator asks. For South African businesses under POPIA, FAIS, or SARB
oversight, the difference between a story and a record is the difference between
a clean audit and a fine."* Then the arc.
