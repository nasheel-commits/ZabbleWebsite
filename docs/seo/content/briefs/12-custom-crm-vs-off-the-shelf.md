## Answer-first intro spec
*"A bespoke CRM is worth building when your sales process doesn't fit the stages,
automations and channels an off-the-shelf CRM assumes — and you're paying in
re-typed quotes, forgotten site visits and a pipeline that lives in three places
that don't agree. If a standard CRM fits how you actually sell, buy it. If you're
bending your team to the tool, build."* Then the arc.
