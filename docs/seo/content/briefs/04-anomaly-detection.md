## Answer-first intro spec
*"Anomaly detection is a system watching a stream of activity in the background
and flagging what's unusual — the fraud, the error, the drift, the outlier —
before it becomes a problem. In a business, fraud, equipment failure and
process drift share one shape: a tiny signal hiding inside an ocean of normal
activity."* Then the arc.
