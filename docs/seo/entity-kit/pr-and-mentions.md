exact pages generative engines already cite when asked "who builds business
automation / custom software in South Africa". Below are those pages (from the
baseline `_evidence/07/llm-response__*` captures), with the ask for each.

## B. Earned mentions (independent corroboration for Wikidata + GEO)

- A short founder/company profile in a South African tech outlet (Ventureburn,
  Disrupt Africa, ITWeb, MyBroadband company news) — one independent article is
  enough to reference the Wikidata item and to corroborate the entity for LLMs.
- A guest article or case study on a SA business/finance publication, bylined,
  using the boilerplate in the author bio.
- Partner co-marketing with **KnvilLabs** (named build partner on Kairos) — a
  reciprocal link/mention corroborates the entity.
